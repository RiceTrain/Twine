You find yourself walking a familiar path through the City. The grey, overbearing clouds colour the world around you. The dreariness makes you feel the craving for something different. Almost as if to answer your mind's call, a flash of light emanates from an Alleyway you were just short of passing by. You find yourself momentarily blinded, but you quickly recover your sight. After some trepidation you slowly peer around the corner. Only a wall of smoke awaits you at first. You cough and splutter while it clears, keeping an eye on a faint green light you can make out on the other side. The smoke's eventual dissipation reveals the source of the glow. Though you remember this Alleyway as empty and featureless, a charming store front appears to have taken its place within it. The eerie light that caught your eye casts odd shadows via the unusual window displays. The building doesn't look like it belongs in this time. Curiousity gets the better of you as you find yourself walking up to door. A sign hangs in the door window that reads "We're open!". You wonder if it's a good idea to just stroll into such a strange place. [[Enter the nameless Shop->Potion seller]] [[Walk Away->Ending 1 - Walk Away]]Despite your instincts about how bad an idea this is, you can't help but take a look inside. You cautiously open the door to let yourself in. Surrounding you are row upon row of bottles, flasks and jars. You find yourself taken aback by the amount of shapes, sizes and colours on offer. The Oil Lantern's flames make elements of the room dance around, flickering shadows everywhere you look. You hear grumbling. You spot a figure hunched over the counter. You guess that it's an old man from the grumbling and you can make out a tall figure with one of the bushiest beards you've seen. You can only just make out some strange robes on him. "I can't believe I've got the wrong bloody location again. I'll soon be fired if I don't sort this out quick. The last thing I need is any old curious so and so waltzing in and..." The old man's eyes meet yours. An awkward pause is swiftly followed by the old man's awkward smile. He almost explodes in sentence. "Welcome to the finest fragrance emporium in...um...this location! Can I interest you in..." The old man's eyes dart about. They eventually rest on something on the shelves. "How about this one? The smell on it is captivating!" Before you think to say anything he had already grabbed the bottle, opened it and had gotten uncomfortably close. "Go on, have a whiff. It'll do you no harm!"
